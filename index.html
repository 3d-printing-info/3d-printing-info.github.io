Introduction

In the depths of the basement of a home, a 3D printer slowly churns out intriguing and intricate objects. Similarly, all across the world, 3D printers are printing the future. Throughout the past several decades, 3D printing has had rich yet rocky growth. Many scientists and engineers have made crucial contributions to the discoveries behind 3D printing. Additionally, 3D printing allows hobbyists and individuals to bring their ideas to life in a tangible form. It is also opening up the doors for an expedited commercial space. Worldwide, 3D printing technologies have been tremendously improved and widely commercialized.
